<p><p>An Active Harmonic Filter is a power electronics device that is used to eliminate low order harmonics from electrical systems, thereby improving power quality. It actively monitors the current waveforms and dynamically injects equal and opposite harmonic currents to cancel out the harmonic distortion. Active Harmonic Filters are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, oil and gas, automotive, and healthcare to maintain power quality, reduce energy losses, and comply with regulatory standards.</p><p>The Active Harmonic Filter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period. Low-voltage active harmonic filters are suitable for smaller electrical systems like residential buildings or small commercial spaces.
