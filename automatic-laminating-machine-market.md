<p><p>Automatic laminating machines can be classified into two main types: coating laminating machines and pre-coated laminating machines. Coating laminating machines apply a layer of adhesive coating onto the material before lamination, while pre-coated laminating machines use pre-coated films or sheets for lamination. Both types of machines are widely used in various industries for enhancing the durability and appearance of printed materials such as labels, packaging, and promotional materials. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the lamination process.</p></p>
