<p><strong>The Balls Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ball Screw</li><li>External Circulation Ball Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screws are mechanical devices used to convert rotational motion into linear motion. The internal circulation ball screw consists of internal ball bearings that circulate within the nut, offering smoother and more efficient operation. On the other hand, the external circulation ball screw has ball bearings that circulate outside the nut, providing higher load capacity and longer service life. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and robotics, offering precise and reliable linear motion control.</p></p>
