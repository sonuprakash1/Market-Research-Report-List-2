<p><p>Bench-top automated immunoassay analyzers are commonly used in laboratories for various types of immunoassay testing. The market types include chemiluminescence analysis, fluorescent immunoassay, radioimmunoassay, and enzyme immunoassay. Chemiluminescence analysis utilizes light produced from a chemical reaction to detect analytes. Fluorescent immunoassay uses fluorescence to detect analytes. Radioimmunoassay involves the use of radioactive isotopes for detection. Enzyme immunoassay utilizes enzymes for detection. Each type offers specific benefits and is used depending on the requirements of the testing process.</p></p>
