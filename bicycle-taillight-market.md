<p><p>Bicycle taillights are available in two main types: self-generating lights and battery-powered lights. Self-generating lights use the motion of the wheels to generate power, eliminating the need for batteries and ensuring continuous operation. On the other hand, battery-powered lights rely on rechargeable or disposable batteries for power. While self-generating lights are more environmentally friendly and cost-effective in the long run, battery-powered lights offer greater flexibility and convenience, as they can be easily removed and replaced.</p></p>
