ELISA method is widely used and offers high sensitivity and specificity. Immunoturbidimetric method is commonly used in clinical laboratories due to its ease of use and automated nature. CLIA method provides rapid results and is suitable for high-throughput testing. Other methods include immunofluorescence and immunochromatographic assays, which offer varying levels of sensitivity and specificity for CRP testing.</p></p>
