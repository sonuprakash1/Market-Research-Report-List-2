<p><p>Colon hydrotherapy machines can be categorized into two main types: open system and closed system. Open system machines are designed for self-administration, with the individual controlling the flow of water into their colon. Closed system machines, on the other hand, are typically used in clinical settings by trained professionals, where the water and waste are contained in a closed system. Both types of machines provide colon cleansing benefits, but the choice between them depends on individual preferences and needs.</p></p>
