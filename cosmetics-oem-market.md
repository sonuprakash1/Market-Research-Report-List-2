<p><ul><li>All process OEM</li><li>Half process OEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetics OEM market types can be classified as All process OEM and Half process OEM. All process OEM involves the manufacturer being responsible for the entire production process, from formulation to packaging. On the other hand, Half process OEM involves the manufacturer taking care of certain stages of production, such as formulation or packaging, while outsourcing other stages to third-party suppliers. Both types allow brands to leverage the expertise and resources of OEM manufacturers to bring high-quality cosmetic products to the market efficiently.</p></p>
