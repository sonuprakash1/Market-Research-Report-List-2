<p><p>Crystal clock oscillators are electronic devices used in various applications to generate precise frequencies. The market for crystal clock oscillators can be divided into two major types based on their mounting method: SMD/SMT and Through Hole. SMD/SMT oscillators are surface-mounted devices that are compact and ideal for high-density circuit boards. Through Hole oscillators, on the other hand, are mounted through holes on the PCB and are known for their durability and stability. Both types cater to different requirements and applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
