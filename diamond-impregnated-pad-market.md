<p><p>Diamond impregnated pads are categorized based on their grit levels, with below 500 grit pads being the coarsest, 500-1500 grit pads falling in the medium range, and above 1500 grit pads being the finest. Below 500 grit pads are ideal for heavy-duty grinding and polishing tasks, while 500-1500 grit pads are suitable for general polishing and refinishing. Above 1500 grit pads are used for achieving a high-gloss finish and are commonly used in the final stages of polishing. Each type of pad serves a specific purpose in the restoration and maintenance of various surfaces.</p></p>
