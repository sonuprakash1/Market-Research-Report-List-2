<p><p>Direct Attach Cable Market types include QSFP (Quad Small Form-factor Pluggable), SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable), CXP, Cx4, CFP (C Form-factor Pluggable), and CDFP. QSFP cables provide high-speed connectivity for data centers and networking applications. SFP cables are compact and versatile, suitable for Ethernet and Fibre Channel connections. CXP cables offer high-density connectivity for high-performance computing. Cx4 cables are used for data transmission in InfiniBand applications. CFP cables are designed for high-speed networking, while CDFP cables offer enhanced performance for data center applications.</p></p>
