<p><strong>The Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode DTS</li><li>Single-Mode DTS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) involves monitoring temperature along a fiber optic cable. Multi-Mode DTS can measure temperature at multiple points simultaneously, making it suitable for complex environments. Single-Mode DTS, on the other hand, focuses on precise temperature measurement at a single location. Multi-Mode DTS is commonly used in industries like oil & gas, while Single-Mode DTS is preferred for more localized monitoring in applications like building management. Both types offer unique benefits depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
