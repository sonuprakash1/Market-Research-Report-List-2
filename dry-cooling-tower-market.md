<p><p>Dry cooling towers are used in industries to remove waste heat generated during various processes. There are two main types of dry cooling towers: open and closed cooling towers. </p><p>Open cooling towers use the ambient air to cool the circulating water through direct contact, whereas closed cooling towers utilize a heat exchanger to transfer heat from the water to the air without any direct contact. </p><p>Both types of towers are efficient in dissipating heat and reducing water consumption, making them popular choices in industrial applications.</p></p>
