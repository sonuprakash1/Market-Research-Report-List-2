<p><strong>The Drying Systems for Softgels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tumbler Dryer & Drying Tunnels</li><li>In-Line Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drying systems for softgels market offers two main types of technologies: Tumbler Dryers and Drying Tunnels. Tumbler Dryers are used for small to medium scale production, providing gentle and consistent drying through a rotating drum. Drying Tunnels, on the other hand, are suitable for high volume production, utilizing a conveyor belt system to move softgels through a controlled drying environment. In-Line Drying systems offer a more streamlined approach, integrating drying processes directly into the production line for increased efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
