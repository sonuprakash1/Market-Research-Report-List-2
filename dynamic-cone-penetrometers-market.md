<p><p>Dynamic Cone Penetrometers (DCP) are used for measuring the strength of soil in geotechnical engineering. The Single-Mass DCP consists of a hammer with a single mass while the Dual-Mass DCP has two masses. The Single-Mass DCP is more commonly used for shallow soil testing, while the Dual-Mass DCP is preferred for deeper soil testing. Both types of DCPs provide valuable data on soil compaction, bearing capacity, and overall soil strength, helping engineers and construction professionals make informed decisions on construction projects.</p></p>
