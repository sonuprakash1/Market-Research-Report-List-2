<p><p>Financial accounting software can be divided into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based financial accounting software is hosted on remote servers accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premise financial accounting software is installed on the company's own servers and computers, providing more control over data security and customization. Both types of software cater to different needs and preferences of organizations, depending on their size, budget, and technology infrastructure.</p></p>
