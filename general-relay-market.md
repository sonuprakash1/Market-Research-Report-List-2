<p><p>General relays are electromagnetic switches used to control circuits in various applications. They come in two main types: DC relays, which are designed to operate in direct current circuits, and AC relays, which are used in alternating current circuits. DC relays are commonly used in automotive and marine applications, while AC relays are used in household appliances and industrial machinery. Both types of relays play a crucial role in controlling the flow of electricity and ensuring the safety and efficiency of electrical systems.</p></p>
