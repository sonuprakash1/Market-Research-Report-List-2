<p><p>Glass Reactors market includes various types such as Thin Film Evaporators, Short Path Evaporators, Filter Reactors, Pressure Reactors, and Rotary Evaporators. Thin Film Evaporators are used for continuous evaporation of heat-sensitive materials. Short Path Evaporators are suitable for separating high boiling point substances. Filter Reactors combine the functions of a filter and a reactor. Pressure Reactors are designed for high-pressure chemical reactions. Rotary Evaporators are used for gentle evaporation of solvents. Each type caters to specific requirements in research, pharmaceuticals, and industrial applications.</p></p>
