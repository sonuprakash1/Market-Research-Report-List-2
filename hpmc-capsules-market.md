<p><strong>The HPMC Capsules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HPMC with Gelling Agent</li><li>HPMC without Gelling Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HPMC capsules are available in two main types - with gelling agent and without gelling agent. HPMC with gelling agent capsules dissolve and release their contents quickly when in contact with gastric fluids, making them suitable for immediate release formulations. On the other hand, HPMC without gelling agent capsules do not require a gelling agent for disintegration and dissolution, providing flexibility in formulation and customization of release profiles. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific needs of the formulation and desired release characteristics.</p></p>
