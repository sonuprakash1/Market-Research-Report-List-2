<p><p>Insulated tools are tools that are designed to provide protection against electric shocks while working on live electrical equipment. These tools are typically made with non-conductive materials such as rubber or plastic to prevent the flow of electric current through the user's body. Insulated tools are commonly used in industries such as electrical, construction, and maintenance to ensure the safety of workers and prevent accidents.</p><p>The Insulated Tools Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period.
