<p><p>Inverting regulators are commonly categorized into two market types: Integrated Switch and External Switch. Integrated Switch regulators incorporate the switching elements within the IC, providing a compact solution with fewer external components required. On the other hand, External Switch regulators utilize an external switch component, offering higher flexibility and customization options. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as space constraints, cost considerations, and required features for the specific application.</p></p>
