<p><ul><li>Laser Level</li><li>Y-level</li><li>Tilting Level</li><li>Automatic Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Levelling instruments market can be divided into four main types: Laser Level, Y-level, Tilting Level, and Automatic Level. Laser levels use laser technology for accurate measurements, Y-levels are used for leveling over long distances, tilting levels have a built-in compensator for adjusting the line of sight, and automatic levels provide self-leveling capabilities. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in terms of accuracy, ease of use, and precision in levelling tasks.</p></p>
