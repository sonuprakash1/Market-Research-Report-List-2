<p><p>Marine cables market includes two main types: fiber-optic cables and electric cables. Fiber-optic cables are used for transmitting data over long distances with high speed and low latency, making them ideal for telecommunications and internet connectivity in marine environments. Electric cables, on the other hand, are used for transmitting electrical power for various marine applications such as powering offshore wind farms, oil rigs, and subsea equipment. Both types of cables play a crucial role in enabling communication and efficient operations in the marine industry.</p></p>
