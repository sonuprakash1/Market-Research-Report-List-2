<p><strong>The Mechanical Torque Limiter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Torque Limiter</li><li>Friction Torque Limiter</li><li>Pneumatic Torque Limiter</li><li>Push/Pull Torque Limiter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical torque limiters come in various types to suit different applications. The ball torque limiter uses balls to transmit torque and disengage when the set torque limit is reached. Friction torque limiters rely on friction to limit torque, while pneumatic torque limiters use air pressure to control torque. Push/pull torque limiters engage/disengage based on axial force applied. Each type offers unique features and benefits to ensure precise torque control and protect machinery from overloading.</p></p>
