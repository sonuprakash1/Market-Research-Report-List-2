<p><p>Micro-Miniature RF connectors are divided into three main market types based on their connection configurations: wire-to-wire RF connectors, wire-to-board RF connectors, and board-to-board RF connectors. Wire-to-wire RF connectors are used to connect two wires together, while wire-to-board RF connectors are used to connect wires to circuit boards. Board-to-board RF connectors are used to connect two circuit boards together. Each type of connector serves a specific purpose in enabling reliable and high-frequency signal transmission in various electronic devices.</p></p>
