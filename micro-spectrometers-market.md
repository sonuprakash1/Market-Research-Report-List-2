<p><p>Micro spectrometers are available in two main types: Chip Type and Modular Type. Chip Type micro spectrometers are integrated into a single chip, making them compact and suitable for portable applications. On the other hand, Modular Type micro spectrometers consist of separate modules that can be customized and configured based on the specific requirements of the user. Both types offer high performance in terms of spectral analysis, but Chip Type spectrometers are more convenient for on-the-go use, while Modular Type spectrometers offer flexibility and adaptability.</p></p>
