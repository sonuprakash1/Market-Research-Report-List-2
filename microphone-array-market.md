<p><p>Microphone Array market consists of various types such as Ceiling Microphone Arrays, Table Microphone Arrays, and Ceiling & Table Microphone Arrays. Ceiling Microphone Arrays are mounted on the ceiling to capture audio from all directions, ideal for conference rooms.
