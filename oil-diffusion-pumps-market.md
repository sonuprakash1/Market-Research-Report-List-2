<p><p>Oil diffusion pumps are commonly used in high vacuum applications to create and maintain a vacuum environment. There are two main types of oil used in these pumps - Silicone oil and Polyphenyl Ethers. </p><p>Silicone oil is widely used for general-purpose high vacuum applications due to its stability and low vapor pressure. On the other hand, Polyphenyl Ethers are preferred for applications requiring higher temperatures and resistance to reactive gases.</p><p>Both types of oil play a crucial role in the efficiency and performance of oil diffusion pumps in various industrial and scientific fields.</p></p>
