<p><p>Organic pea protein is available in three main market types: isolates, concentrates, and textured. Isolates are the most refined form of pea protein, containing over 90% protein content with minimal carbohydrates and fats. Concentrates have a slightly lower protein content, around 80%, but retain more fiber and nutrients. Textured pea protein is processed to have a meat-like texture and is commonly used as a meat alternative in plant-based products. Each market type caters to different consumer preferences and dietary needs.</p></p>
