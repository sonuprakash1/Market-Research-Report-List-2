<p><p>Passive Buzzer market types include stationary and pogo pin variants. Stationary buzzers are fixed in place and typically used in applications where the buzzer does not need to move. Pogo pin buzzers are designed with spring-loaded pins for easy connection and disconnection, making them ideal for applications requiring frequent assembly and disassembly. Both types offer reliable sound output and are commonly used in electronic devices for alerts and notifications.</p></p>
