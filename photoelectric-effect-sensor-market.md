<p><strong>The Photoelectric Effect Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Pulse Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog photoelectric sensors operate by continuously measuring the amount of light received, providing a continuous output signal proportionate to the light intensity. They are ideal for applications requiring precise detection of objects or distances. </p><p>On the other hand, pulse photoelectric sensors use pulses of light to detect objects, providing a digital output signal when an object is detected within a specific range. They are commonly used for applications requiring high-speed object detection or precise distance measurement. Both types of sensors play critical roles in industrial automation and control systems.</p></p>
