<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Removal</li><li>Negative Photoresist Removal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photoresist removal market can be classified into two main types based on the method used for removal: positive photoresist removal and negative photoresist removal. Positive photoresist removal involves removing the areas of photoresist that were exposed to light during the photolithography process, leaving behind the desired pattern. On the other hand, negative photoresist removal involves removing the areas of photoresist that were not exposed to light, resulting in the desired pattern. Both types of photoresist removal play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry for the fabrication of microchips and electronic devices.</p></p>
