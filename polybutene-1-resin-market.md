Homopolymer Type refers to a polymer composed of only polybutene-1 repeat units, resulting in a uniform structure. On the other hand, Random Copolymer Type contains a combination of polybutene-1 and other monomers, leading to a more diverse structure. These different types of polybutene-1 resins offer varying properties and applications in industries such as packaging, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
