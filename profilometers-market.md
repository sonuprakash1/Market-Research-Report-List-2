<p><ul><li>Contact Profilometer</li><li>Non-Contact Profilometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Profilmeters are used in various industries for measuring surface profiles. Contact profilometers physically touch the surface to measure its profile, while non-contact profilometers use optical, laser, or stylus technology to measure without touching the surface. Contact profilometers are more common in industries such as manufacturing, where high precision is required, while non-contact profilometers are preferred in industries like electronics and medical devices where surface damage must be minimized. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring product quality and performance.</p></p>
