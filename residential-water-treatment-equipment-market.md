<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU</li><li>POE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential water treatment equipment market includes Point-of-Use (POU) and Point-of-Entry (POE) systems. POU systems are installed at individual fixtures to provide localized treatment, such as under-the-sink filters. POE systems are installed at the main water line to treat water for the entire household, like whole-house filtration systems. POU systems are popular for their convenience and cost-effectiveness, while POE systems are preferred for comprehensive water treatment for the entire household. Both types of systems help improve water quality and ensure safe drinking water.</p></p>
