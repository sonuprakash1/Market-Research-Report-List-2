<p><ul><li>Centralized Dispensing Systems</li><li>Decentralized Dispensing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centralized Dispensing Systems involve a central pharmacy location where medications are stored and dispensed automatically. This system is typically used in larger healthcare facilities where a high volume of prescriptions are processed. Decentralized Dispensing Systems, on the other hand, involve smaller automated dispensing units placed throughout a healthcare facility, allowing for medications to be dispensed closer to patients. Decentralized systems are often used in smaller facilities or facilities with different departments requiring easy access to medications.</p></p>
