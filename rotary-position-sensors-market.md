<p><strong>The Rotary Position Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Rotary Position Sensors</li><li>Non-Contact Rotary Position Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary position sensors are used to measure the angular position of a rotating object. Contact rotary position sensors make physical contact with the object to determine its position, while non-contact rotary position sensors use technologies like magnetic or optical sensors to measure position without touching the object. Contact sensors are typically more durable and cost-effective, while non-contact sensors offer higher accuracy and reliability. Both types cater to different industry needs and applications based on their specific features and advantages.</p></p>
