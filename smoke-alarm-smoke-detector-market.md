<p><strong>The Smoke Alarm (Smoke Detector) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Ionization</li><li>Dual Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke alarm market is divided into three main types: photoelectric, ionization, and dual sensor detectors. Photoelectric smoke alarms use light beams to detect smoke particles, while ionization alarms use a small amount of radioactive material to ionize the air and detect smoke particles. Dual sensor alarms combine both technologies for increased efficiency in detecting different types of fires. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses in terms of detecting smoldering fires versus flaming fires, allowing consumers to choose the best option for their specific needs.</p></p>
