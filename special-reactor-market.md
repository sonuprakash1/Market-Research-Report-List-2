<p><p>Special reactor market types can be classified into non-shielded reactors and shielded reactors. Non-shielded reactors are those that do not have any outer protective shielding to contain radiation. On the other hand, shielded reactors are equipped with protective shielding materials to prevent radiation leakage. Both types of reactors serve specific purposes in various industries such as nuclear power generation, medical applications, and research. The choice between non-shielded and shielded reactor types depends on the specific requirements and safety considerations of the application.</p></p>
