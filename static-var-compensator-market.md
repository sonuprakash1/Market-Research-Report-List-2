<p><strong>The Static Var Compensator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TCR-based SVC</li><li>MCR-based SVC</li><li>TSC-based SVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static Var Compensator (SVC) market is divided into three main types based on the technology used: Thyristor Controlled Reactor (TCR)-based SVC, Mechanically Controlled Reactor (MCR)-based SVC, and Thyristor Switched Capacitor (TSC)-based SVC. TCR-based SVCs use thyristors to control the reactor impedance, MCR-based SVCs use mechanically controlled reactors, and TSC-based SVCs use thyristors to switch in and out capacitor banks. Each type offers different advantages and is suited to different applications based on their specific technology and control capabilities.</p></p>
