<p><p>The Synthetic Insulators market can be divided into different types based on their design and usage. Suspension Synthetic Insulators are used to hang and support electrical lines, while Line Post Synthetic Insulators are installed on utility poles for supporting conductors. Braced Line Post Synthetic Insulators are similar to Line Post insulators but provide additional stability through braces.
