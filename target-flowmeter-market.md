<p><p>The Target Flowmeter Market offers various types of flowmeters including Shaft Seal Diaphragm Structure, Flexible Tube Structure, Torsional Tube Structure, and Differential Pressure Target Structure. Shaft Seal Diaphragm Structure flowmeters use a sealed diaphragm to measure flow. Flexible Tube Structure flowmeters utilize a flexible tube to measure flow. Torsional Tube Structures measure flow using a twisted tube mechanism. Differential Pressure Target Structure flowmeters measure flow by detecting pressure differences. Each type offers different benefits and applications for measuring flow in various industries.</p></p>
