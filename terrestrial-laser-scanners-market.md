<p><strong>The Terrestrial Laser Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase Based Scanners</li><li>Time-Of-Flight Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Terrestrial Laser Scanners are used for creating 3D models of objects or landscapes in various industries. Two main types of scanners are Phase Based scanners and Time-Of-Flight scanners. Phase Based scanners measure the distance by calculating the phase shift of the laser beam, while Time-Of-Flight scanners measure the distance by calculating the time taken for the laser beam to reflect back to the scanner. Both types are widely used in industries such as construction, engineering, and archaeology for accurate and detailed 3D scanning.</p></p>
