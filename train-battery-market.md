<p><p>Train battery market encompasses three main types, namely lead acid battery, nickel cadmium battery, and lithium ion battery. Lead acid batteries are most commonly used due to their cost-effectiveness and reliability. Nickel cadmium batteries offer high performance and long lifespan but are being phased out due to environmental concerns. Lithium ion batteries are gaining popularity due to their high energy density and longer cycle life. They are also lightweight and require less maintenance, making them ideal for use in trains.</p></p>
