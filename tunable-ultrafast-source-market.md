<p><p>The tunable ultrafast source market includes femtosecond laser sources and picosecond laser sources. Femtosecond laser sources produce extremely short pulses of light in the femtosecond range (1 quadrillionth of a second), ideal for applications in material processing, biomedical imaging, and scientific research. Picosecond laser sources generate pulses in the picosecond range (1 trillionth of a second), suitable for applications in telecommunications, semiconductor processing, and spectroscopy. Both types of sources offer tunability, high energy output, and precise control for a wide range of ultrafast laser applications.</p></p>
