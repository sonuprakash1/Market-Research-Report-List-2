<p><strong>The UHF RFID Tags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UHF Dry Inlay RFID Tags</li><li>UHF Wet Inlay RFID Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UHF RFID Tags market consists of two main types: UHF Dry Inlay RFID Tags and UHF Wet Inlay RFID Tags. </p><p>UHF Dry Inlay RFID Tags are tags with RFID chips and antennas that are attached to a substrate, such as paper or plastic, without any additional protective covering. These tags are commonly used in applications where the tag will not be exposed to harsh environments.</p><p>UHF Wet Inlay RFID Tags, on the other hand, have a protective layer, typically made of plastic or laminate, that covers the RFID chip and antenna. These tags are more durable and are often used in applications where the tag will be exposed to moisture, chemicals, or physical wear and tear.</p></p>
