<p><p>Vertical Sump Pumps are a type of pump that is used to pump liquid out of a sump or pit. These pumps are designed to be submerged in the liquid, with the motor located above the liquid level to prevent any water damage. Vertical sump pumps are commonly used in applications such as drainage, effluent, wastewater, and industrial processes.</p><p>The market for Vertical Sump Pumps is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period. The key drivers for this growth include increasing urbanization, industrialization, and infrastructure development activities worldwide.
