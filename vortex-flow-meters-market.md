<p><p>Inline Vortex Flow Meters are directly installed in a pipeline and are ideal for continuous flow measurement. Insertion Vortex Flow Meters are inserted into a pipeline through a valve and are suitable for larger pipe sizes where inline meters are not practical. Massflow Vortex Flow Meters are designed to measure the mass flow rate of a fluid rather than the volumetric flow rate. Each type of Vortex Flow Meter has specific applications and benefits depending on the requirements of the flow measurement system.</p></p>
