<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Manipulator</li><li>Vacuum Manipulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer transfer robot market consists of two main types of manipulators: atmospheric manipulators and vacuum manipulators. Atmospheric manipulators are designed to operate in ambient conditions, utilizing mechanical arms to manipulate wafers. Vacuum manipulators, on the other hand, are equipped with suction cups to securely hold and transfer wafers in a controlled vacuum environment. Both types of manipulators are crucial in the semiconductor industry for transferring delicate wafers during the manufacturing process, ensuring precision and efficiency in handling these sensitive components.</p></p>
