<p><p>Wind lidar systems are used for measuring wind speed and direction in the offshore and onshore wind energy industry. There are three main types of wind lidar systems - Nacelle Wind Lidar, Ground-based Wind Lidar, and Others. Nacelle Wind Lidar systems are mounted on the nacelle of a wind turbine, while Ground-based Wind Lidar systems are positioned on the ground. Other types of wind lidar systems include floating lidar and pulsed wind lidar. These systems play a crucial role in optimizing wind farm performance and increasing energy production.</p></p>
